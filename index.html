Data Protection Framework
DPA 2019 · Constitution of Kenya Art. 31 · UN CRC Art. 16
🔐
UCID — Pseudonymisation
The Unique Child Identifier contains zero personally identifiable information. It is cryptographically generated (UUID v4) and irreversibly pseudonymised. No name, birth date, or biometric data is stored.
📋
Data Minimisation (S.25 DPA 2019)
Upon discharge, all remaining descriptive data is purged. Only aggregate, anonymised statistics are retained for research. The discharge action is irreversible and triggers a critical audit entry.
🔒
Access Control & Role Separation
Role-based access control (RBAC) ensures each agency sees only the data relevant to their mandate. Police cannot view judiciary records and vice versa. NCCS has read-only analytics access.
📜
Immutable Audit Trail
Every view, edit, login, and registration is permanently logged with timestamp, session ID, simulated IP, and officer identity. Retention: 10 years per DPA 2019 s.49.
